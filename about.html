About Us

Company History

Furrever Friends was founded in 2019 by Vhic Pauline Resomadero, an animal enthusiast with a dream of creating a sanctuary for pets and pet lovers alike. The inspiration for the pet shop came when Vhic noticed a lack of comprehensive pet care and adoption services in the community. Starting with a cozy storefront, Vhic's dedication to animal welfare quickly resonated with locals, and the shop became known for its exceptional care and wide selection of pet supplies.

In just a few years, Furrever Friends expanded to a larger space, offering an even greater variety of pets, products, and services. Vhic introduced wellness clinics, training sessions, and community events to promote responsible pet ownership and strengthen the bond between pets and their families. Today, Furrever Friends is a beloved fixture in the community, known for its warm atmosphere, knowledgeable staff, and unwavering commitment to finding loving homes for every animal.
